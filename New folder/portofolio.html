Kurumi Tokosaki

Kurumi Tokisaki (時とき崎さき 狂くる三み, Tokisaki Kurumi?) is the third Spirit to appear. Due to her brutal actions, she is referred to as the Worst Spirit (最悪の精霊, Saiaku no Seirei?). She is also the first Spirit to appear as an antagonist in the Date A Live series.

This article deals with the original Kurumi Tokisaki. For the Kurumi that appears in Date A Bullet, please go to the Kurumi Tokisaki (Date A Bullet) article.
